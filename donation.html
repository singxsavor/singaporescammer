This Vesak Day, Give Light, Dignity & Blessings

Be the light in someone’s darkness. One act of compassion can ripple across lifetimes.

On this sacred day of compassion and reflection, Toa Payoh Seu Teck Sean Tong invites you to take part in a meaningful act of giving. Every year, elderly individuals in Singapore pass away alone — with no family, no funds, and no proper farewell. For decades, we’ve quietly provided dignified funeral rites to the underprivileged. But rising costs and limited resources now threaten this mission.

Your donation helps ensure no one’s final journey is left incomplete — and earns you blessings, spiritual merit, and peace of heart.

SCAM ALERT: KL Transportation Services

UEN: 53444733C | Singapore

Company Overview

KL Transportation Services is a sole proprietorship registered in Singapore with the Unique Entity Number (UEN) 53444733C. Officially listed under ACRA, the company claims to offer moving and delivery services.

  • Name of Business: KL Transportation Services
  • UEN: 53444733C
  • Registration Date: 02 January 2022
  • Commencement Date: 02 January 2022
  • Status of Business: Live
  • Renewal Date: 29 November 2023
  • Expiry Date: 02 January 2027
  • Business Constitution: Sole-Proprietor
  • Principal Place of Business: 352C Canberra Road #08-237, Singapore 753352
  • Primary Activity: Moving Services (SSIC Code: 49232)
  • User-Described Activity: Delivery Services, Furniture Moving Services

Authorities' Response

Police investigations are ongoing. KL Transportation Services’ UEN has reportedly been frozen. Public advised to avoid further transactions.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Do not pay upfront unless verified.
  • Always check reviews and ScamSG listings.
  • Report incidents to Singapore Police.

Scam Allegations and Reports

Warning: Numerous victims have come forward on ScamSG.com, alleging that KL Transportation Services is involved in fraudulent activities, including:
  • Collecting payment for delivery or disposal services and failing to fulfill the agreement.
  • Selling customers' furniture on Carousell without consent.
  • Using fake online profiles and impersonation to discredit whistleblowers.
  • Running a deceptive "freecycling" scam — promising free items, then charging for non-existent deliveries.
